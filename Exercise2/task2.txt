Pseudocode for task 2

User inputs the number of accepted exercises 
and the program prints out their grade.

User input: integer(Exercise amount)

if Exercise amount is lower than 9:
    print: You have failed the course due to lack of completed exercises.
elif Exercise amount is 9:
    print: Your grade is 1.
elif Exercise amount is 10:
    print: Your grade is 2.
elif Exercise amount is 11:
    print: Your grade is 3.
elif Exercise amount is 12:
    print: Your grade is 4.
elif Exercise amount is 13 or higher:
    print: Your grade is 5.
else:
    print: Incorrect input, unable to provide grade.
